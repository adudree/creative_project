How to use Melod'IMAC?

  1. Compose your own melody
  2. Listen to it
  3. Let the magic AI compose the end of the music
  4. Enjoy!

Let's compose!

  1. Click on "Start composing"
  2. Compose your own melody (8 notes) by playing with your keyboard. You can use the GUI parameters to chose the length of the notes that you want to write, the numbers of notes that you want the AI to find, and the random degree for the AI.
  3. Click on "End composing" if you have finished.
